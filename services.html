What We Offer

Our Services

End-to-end interior solutions under one roof — from concept drawings to complete execution with the finest craftsmanship.

01
2D / 3D Drawings

Before a single piece of material is sourced, we produce detailed shop drawings and photorealistic 3D rendered designs so you can see your space come alive. This ensures there are no surprises — only excitement on handover day.

  • Detailed shop drawings
  • Photorealistic 3D renders
  • Elevation and plan views
  • Material specification sheets
Design First
02
Joinery Works

Custom furniture, cabinetry, doors, and woodwork crafted with precision in our workshop. Every joint, edge, and finish is a reflection of our dedication to craftsmanship. We work with premium-grade MDF, plywood, and solid wood.

  • Custom wardrobes & cabinets
  • Modular furniture
  • Doors & partitions
  • Decorative woodwork & panelling
Craftsmanship
03
Corian & Stone

Premium solid surfaces and natural stone installations that elevate kitchens, bathrooms, and feature walls. We work with Corian, marble, granite, quartzite, and engineered stone — sourced from the finest suppliers.

  • Kitchen countertops
  • Bathroom vanity tops
  • Feature wall cladding
  • Reception & lobby surfaces
Premium Stone
04
Electric Work

Wiring, lighting installation, and complete electrical solutions designed for both beauty and safety. Our team handles concealed conduit work, feature lighting coves, chandeliers, sensor systems, and full DB installations.

  • Concealed wiring & conduit
  • Cove & feature lighting
  • DB & switchboard setup
  • Smart lighting controls
Electrical
05
Glass & Aluminum Work

Bespoke glass partitions, aluminium cladding, frameless shower enclosures, structural glazing, and modern façade solutions. We bring light and openness into spaces without compromising on privacy or structure.

  • Frameless glass partitions
  • Aluminium cladding & trims
  • Shower enclosures
  • Structural glazing
Glass & Aluminium
06
False Ceiling & Wall Paint

Decorative false ceilings, coffered designs, and premium wall paints to define ambiance and character. We work with gypsum, POP, grid, and wood-look ceilings, finished with curated colour palettes that complement your interior.

  • Gypsum & POP false ceilings
  • Coffered & cove designs
  • Premium wall paint
  • Texture & specialty finishes
Ceilings & Finishes
07
Upholstery & Curtains

Luxury fabric and leather upholstery for furniture and walls, paired with bespoke curtain solutions that bring warmth, texture, and elegance to every room. We source from premium fabric houses across India.

  • Sofa & chair reupholstery
  • Leather & fabric wall panels
  • Custom curtains & drapes
  • Roman blinds & shutters
  • Headboard & bed upholstery
  • Cushions & soft furnishings
Soft Furnishings
